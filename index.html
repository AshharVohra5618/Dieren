About us

Animal Place

You’ve thought it through carefully: you’re getting a pet! Animals of all shapes and sizes are offered on Dierenplaats. Both breeders and private individuals are carefully screened, checked, and selected on Dierenplaats. This ensures that all animals find a good home! The goal of Dierenplaats is to match animals with a new owner and to find a suitable owner for your animal. When purchasing an animal, you must, of course, take certain circumstances into account. What kind of animal are you looking for? What is your living situation? Do your personalities match? What requirements do you have for the animal, and which animal suits you?

Why

Choose Dieren Plats?

Why choose Dierenplaats? You love animals and are looking for the perfect match. Dieren-Plaats helps you find a companion for life. We consider it only natural that your future companion comes from a good litter. Dierenplaats is the animal platform where both breeders and private individuals can buy or sell animals. The breeders active on Dierenplaats have been rated by animal lovers based on experience and expertise. This way, we help each other put an end to puppy mills. You can post a review for your breeder and read reviews from others, so that you can make a well-considered decision when purchasing your new housemate.
